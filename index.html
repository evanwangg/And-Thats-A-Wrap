Music Consumption Methods: Revenue Proportions

Each ring represents a year. The innermost ring starts at 1999 and increases by year until the outermost ring at 2023. Each ring shows the proportion of revenue for each consumption method (Physical, Downloads, and Streaming) for that year. Hovering over a ring displays a tooltip with detailed information about revenue for that year.

Clicking the buttons allows you to see the revenues for the different consumption methods.
